How It Works
    • Conceptualization
    The community can submit designs through our platform to be evaluated, and once designs and concepts have been deemed suitable, we cast a public poll that allows the community to vote on their favorite designs.
    Development
    Once the design has been popularized through public polling we work with the designer on further developing their concepts by incorporating the proper materials and technologies.
    Execution
    With the concept evaluated and materials selected we can create a production quality prototype and set it up for crowdfunding. We do it for the artists - all designs are credited to their designers.
